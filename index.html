Our Process

Precision at
Every Step

🎨

CAD Design

Our in-house CAD artist translates your vision into a precise 3D blueprint — every angle, every facet perfected digitally first.

🖨️

3D Wax Printing

Using our Phrozen 12K printer, we produce a flawless wax model with micron-level accuracy before a single gram of gold is cast.

🔥

Casting & Filing

Master casters pour precious metal. Expert artisans then hand-file and refine every edge to achieve surgical precision.

💎

Stone Setting

Certified diamond setters place every stone with absolute care. Final polishing brings out the diamond's maximum brilliance.
