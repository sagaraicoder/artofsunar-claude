Our Story

Craft Meets Technology

At Art of Sunar, we marry centuries-old goldsmithing tradition with modern 3D design and printing technology. Our skilled team — from CAD artists to master casting artisans — brings every design to life with unmatched precision.

Using the Phrozen 12K 3D printer, we achieve detail impossible by hand alone, then finish each piece with traditional filing, polishing, and stone setting artistry.

How We Work

From Vision to Reality

01
Consultation

Share your idea, reference photos, or sketch with us via WhatsApp or our enquiry form. We discuss your vision, metal preference, budget, and timeline.

02
CAD Design

Our CAD artist creates a photorealistic 3D design for your approval. You see exactly how it will look before any metal is used.

03
3D Print & Cast

Approved design is printed on our Phrozen 12K printer, then expertly cast in gold or silver by our artisan team.

04
Finish & Deliver

Filing, polishing, and stone setting bring the piece to perfection. Your jewellery is delivered with care and quality assurance.
